
This is the wiring hookup as seen in the Adafruit Learn guide.

	https://learn.adafruit.com/animated-electronic-eyes-using-teensy-3-1/overview#wiring

For TFT LCD displays, use these connections:

Vin to BAT+ (if LiPoly) or USB+
Gnd to GND
SCK to SPI CLK
SI to SPI MOSI
TCS to Digital Pin 9 (left eye) or 10 (right)
RST to Digital Pin 8
D/C to Digital Pin 7
